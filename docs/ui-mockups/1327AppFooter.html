AppFooter Component Showcase

This page demonstrates the AppFooter organism. It is designed to be placed at the bottom of the application layout, pushed down by the main content.

On mobile screens (below 768px), the copyright and navigation links are stacked vertically in the center. On larger screens, they are arranged horizontally, with the copyright on the left and links on the right.
